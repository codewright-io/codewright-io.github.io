Who are we?

A co-operative of very experienced developers based in Sydney, Australia, who have worked together for over a decade on large commercial projects. We're friends with a shared passion for software development, technology and start-up culture. We enjoy each other's company and enjoy working together

We’re those star-developers on a team that dive head-first into the complex & architectural parts of a project. We have a diverse range of skills, and a passion for clean, maintainable, reliable code

Where are we a good fit?

We're looking to build close, lasting relationships with a few key customers - We aim to provide the advantages of a reliable, in-house developers with the scaling flexibilty of temporary developers

Here's where we fit well:

  • Bootstrapping and architecting a new project to pass on to some less-experienced developers
  • Coming in and cleaning up an older complex system. Moving it to a more maintainable code base. We can help your team develop ownership of their code
  • Help with moving from a monolith architecture to microservices
  • A few solid on-shore developers to assist an off-shore team
  • Key industries: IoT, Web, Highly scalable, high demand systems, event-driven systems

What is a developer co-operative?

As a co-operative the developer that does the work gets the rate directly, so there’s no management overhead. All members all have equal say in how the co-operative is run

We aim to give back to the community, so encourage members to spend a percentage of our time on projects that will improve the local community & world that we all live in

We each maintain our own insurance & equipment. We have shared office space or can work on site as needed.

We're all the same price per head, but will help each other out on projects as needed - Get a huge range of skills & experience for the price of one or two developers!

Hire a developer for as much or as little time as you require, avoid ongoing commitments
