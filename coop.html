Aims for Customer Relationships

  • Build a close working relationship with a customer, and before committing to work together making sure that we are a right-fit for each other
  • No business conflicts with existing customers
  • Help in cases of developers getting sick, needing a holiday, etc… so that customers are not left in the lurch - Maybe a shadow developer for a time
  • Commit to work with customers closely and help transition on new teams (remote or local) as needed - Never leave customers in the lurch

Aims for Members

  • Collaboration
    • Discussion on each others projects, work with each other
    • Open forum & chat/video channels
    • Work together on social projects, that give back to the community
    • Friday afternoon unstructured discussion on our current projects
    • Bi-monthly structured discussions about current projects and interesting technologies
  • Shared resources where available (Servers, equipment, etc…)
  • Development of shared framework libraries to boot strap projects

Conditions of Active Membership

  • A small yearly fee of $10
  • Continued sponsorship by another active member
  • Active insurance if undertaking active customer work

Membership Rules

  • Primary Activities: Provide outsourced development services, develop our own projects. Support community and socially ethical projects
  • Positions: President (Runs meetings), Secretary (Records minutes), Treasurer (Manage finances). All other members are board-members
  • Election of positions by an annual vote
  • Resolution of changes to rules by 80% majority, or can pass on 60% majority on two consecutive votes between two to four weeks apart. Voting done by email, with open results
  • All members must have insurance to work on a contract
