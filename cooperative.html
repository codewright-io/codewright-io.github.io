Co-operative

What is a developer co-operative?

A developer co-operative is run democratically by the developers involved, with everyone getting an equal say on how the co-operative is run.

Hire a developer for as much or as little time as you require, avoid ongoing commitments



Benefits

All rates go directly to the developers with very little overheads. We all maintain our own equipment & insurance, and have shared officce space to use or can work on site as required.



Community

We aim to give back to the community, so encourage members to spend a percentage of our time on projects that will improve the local community & world that we all live in.
